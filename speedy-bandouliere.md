# Louis Vuitton Speedy Bandoulière 30 Review & Authentication 2026

The Louis Vuitton Speedy Bandoulière 30 is a timeless classic. This guide covers the 2026 version, authentication, and premium alternatives.

## Authentication Checklist

### Canvas & Pattern

1. **Pattern alignment**: Monogram/Damier must align perfectly at seams
2. **Corner leather**: Should have clean edges, slight patina on vachetta
3. **Glazing**: Consistent width, no peeling

### Hardware

- **Lock**: Golden brass with "Louis Vuitton" stamp
- **Keys**: Come with clochette (key holder)
- ** zipper**: Smooth Riri or Lampo zipper

### Stitching

- 10 stitches per inch minimum
- Matching thread color throughout
- No loose threads

### Interior

- Microfiber lining (brown or beige)
- "Louis Vuitton" made in stamp
- Date code present

**Price Range**: $350-$600

## Authentication Points That Matter

1. **Date code** - Two letters + numbers, correct position
2. **Heat stamp depth** - Consistent, not too deep or shallow
3. **Leather tag** - Stitched, not glued
4. **Stitching** - 10 per inch, no variance
5. **Monogram texture** - Embossed, not flat
