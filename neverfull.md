## Authentic vs Premium 1:1 Comparison

### Canvas Quality

**Authentic Louis Vuitton Monogram Canvas:**
- 100% cotton canvas with PVC coating
- Thread weight: consistent 3-ply
- Alignment: perfect at all seams
- Edge coating: smooth, slightly glossy

**Premium 1:1 Quality:**
- 80% cotton canvas with PVC coating (comparable)
- Thread weight: 2-3 ply (very close)
- Alignment: correct at major seams, slight variance at corners
- Edge coating: smooth matte finish

### Hardware

**Authentic:**
- Solid brass with 24k gold plating
- "Louis Vuitton" stamping: clean, consistent depth
- Heat stamps: precise alignment
- Zipper: high-quality Riri or Lampo

**Premium 1:1:**
- Brass with quality gold plating
- "Louis Vuitton" stamping: sharp, slight variance
- Heat stamps: correct position
- Zipper: quality YKK or Riri

### Stitching

- Authentic: 8-10 stitches per inch, alternating direction
- 1:1 Quality: 8 stitches per inch, consistent throughout

## Authentication Guide

### 1. Date Code

Every authentic Louis Vuitton bag has a date code stamped inside:

- Format: Two letters + two numbers (e.g., "SD4199")
- First two letters: factory location
- Last two digits + following two digits: year and month

**2026 Date Codes:**
- CA (France): CA 0226
- FS (France): FS 0426
- BH (USA): BH 0126
- SP (Spain): SP 0326

### 2. Canvas Alignment

The Monogram canvas must flow continuously across all seams:
- Front pocket: pattern aligned
- Side seams: mirror image alignment
- Bottom: symmetrical pattern

### 3. Stitching Quality

Count stitches per inch:
- Authentic: 8-10 at all seams
- Fake: often 6-7 or irregular

### 4. Hardware Details

- Logo engraving: crisp, not cast
- Chain links: even weight
- Lock: smooth mechanism
